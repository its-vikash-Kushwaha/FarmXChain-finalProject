FarmXChain – Blockchain-Based Smart Agriculture Supply Chain Platform
Introduction
FarmXChain is a blockchain-enabled smart agriculture supply chain platform designed to
bring transparency, traceability, and trust to the farm-to-fork ecosystem. The system
connects farmers, distributors, retailers, and consumers on a single digital platform, ensuring
fair pricing, real-time tracking, and immutable records of agricultural produce.
By leveraging Blockchain, IoT, and full-stack web technologies, FarmXChain eliminates
middlemen inefficiencies, prevents data tampering, and empowers farmers with better
market access and insights.
